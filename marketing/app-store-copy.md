## Full description

**Design on an infinite canvas — locally.**

Kirutma is a desktop design application for UI mockups, layouts, and vector graphics. Work with frames, shapes, text, and images on a smooth, GPU-accelerated canvas. Organize your work with layers, pages, color styles, text styles, and reusable components.

**Tools you need**

- Move, Frame, Rectangle, Ellipse, Triangle, Polygon, Star, Pen, Line, Text, Hand, and Scale
- Multi-select, group, ungroup, and transform with handles
- Import images; edit fills, strokes, typography, opacity, and shadows

**Files & export**

- Save and open `.kirutma` documents
- Recent files from the home screen
- Export selection to PNG (1×, 2×, 3×) or SVG

**Built for flow**

- Dark, professional interface
- Keyboard shortcuts for tools, editing, and zoom
- Undo/redo and in-app shortcut reference

Kirutma keeps your work on your machine by default — ideal for focused design sessions without mandatory accounts or cloud uploads.
