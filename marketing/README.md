## Before you publish

1. Replace `[DOWNLOAD_URL]`, `[WEBSITE_URL]`, `[CONTACT_EMAIL]`, and `[GITHUB_URL]` placeholders.
2. Add screenshots to `screenshots/` (see guide there).
3. Confirm license and support channels in FAQ and press kit.
4. Review localized snippets with a native speaker if targeting that market seriously.

## Tone guidelines

- Lead with **what designers can do**, not internal architecture.
- Emphasize **local-first**, **fast canvas**, and **professional workflow**.
- Avoid comparing to other products by name.
- Keep platform references neutral: “desktop app” rather than naming specific operating systems unless required by a store listing.
