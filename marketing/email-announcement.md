Hi `[FIRST_NAME]`,

Today we’re releasing **Kirutma 1.0** — a desktop design application for people who want a fast, focused canvas and files that stay on their own machine.

**What you can do in v1.0**

- Design on an **infinite canvas** with frames, shapes, text, pen, lines, and images  
- Organize work with **layers**, **pages**, **color styles**, **text styles**, and **components**  
- **Save** portable `.kirutma` documents and pick up where you left off from **Recent files**  
- **Export** selections to PNG (1×, 2×, 3×) or SVG  

Kirutma is built for uninterrupted design sessions: dark UI, keyboard shortcuts, undo/redo, and no mandatory cloud account.
