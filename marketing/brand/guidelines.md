# Kirutma Brand Guidelines

Lightweight reference for marketing, social, and partner use.

---

## Name

**Kirutma** — capitalize as shown. Do not abbreviate to “K” in formal copy.

**Pronunciation (guide):** kee-ROOT-mah *(adjust if you prefer a different official pronunciation)*

---

## Logo / app icon

| Asset | File |
|-------|------|
| Primary icon (1024×1024) | [kirutma-icon-1024.png](./kirutma-icon-1024.png) |

### Clear space

Maintain padding equal to **10% of icon width** on all sides when placing on busy backgrounds.

### Minimum size

- **Digital:** 32×32 px (app icon); 64×64 px preferred for social avatars  
- **Print:** 12 mm minimum

### Don’t

- Stretch or squash the icon  
- Add drop shadows or outlines not in the original artwork  
- Place on low-contrast backgrounds that hide detail  
- Rotate the icon for “visual interest”

## Color palette

Derived from the in-app dark theme.

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|--------|
| **Canvas** | `#1E1E1E` | Page backgrounds, hero sections |
| **Panel** | `#2C2C2C` | Cards, secondary surfaces |
| **Input** | `#383838` | Form fields, subtle elevation |
| **Accent** | `#0D99FF` | Primary buttons, links, highlights |
| **Accent hover** | `#0B7FD4` | Hover states |
| **Text primary** | `#FFFFFF` | Headlines, body on dark |
| **Text secondary** | `rgba(255,255,255,0.7)` | Supporting copy |
| **Text muted** | `rgba(255,255,255,0.45)` | Captions, metadata |
| **Border subtle** | `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` | Dividers |

### Light-mode marketing (optional)

For print or light web sections, use white `#FFFFFF` background with canvas `#1E1E1E` text and accent `#0D99FF` for CTAs.

## Typography

**UI & marketing (recommended):** [Inter](https://rsms.me/inter/)  
**System fallbacks:** `-apple-system`, BlinkMacSystemFont, `"Segoe UI"`, sans-serif

| Element | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Hero headline | 32–48 px | 600 (Semibold) |
| Section title | 20–24 px | 600 |
| Body | 14–16 px | 400 |
| Caption | 11–12 px | 400–500 |

## Voice & tone

- **Clear** — say what the tool does in plain language  
- **Confident** — v1.0 is a real product, not a demo  
- **Respectful** — designers’ time and files matter  
- **Honest** — don’t claim features that aren’t shipped  

Avoid naming competing products in official Kirutma materials.

---

## File format notation

Always write **`.kirutma`** with a leading dot and lowercase extension in body copy.

Example: “Save your work as a `.kirutma` file.”

---

## CTA button labels

Preferred: **Download Kirutma**, **Try Kirutma**, **Get v1.0**, **New file** (in-product context)
