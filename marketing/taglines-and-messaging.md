## Elevator pitch (30 seconds)

Kirutma is a desktop design application for people who want a fast, focused canvas without opening a browser. You get frames, shapes, text, layers, reusable components, and style libraries — plus PNG and SVG export. Files are saved as portable `.kirutma` documents on your computer. It is built for solo design work today, with collaboration on the roadmap.

## Words to use

- design tool, infinite canvas, frames, shapes, layers, components, styles
- local-first, offline-capable, portable files, export
- fast, focused, professional, keyboard shortcuts

## Words to avoid

- Comparisons to other products by name
- “Replacement for…” framing (unless you intentionally run a comparison campaign)
- Overpromising features not in v1.0 (real-time multiplayer, plugins, auto layout, etc.)
